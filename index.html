Mission Statement:

The mission of creating this site is to create a website with a broad view on a certain consoles history.

A lot of websites that cover retro games tend to usually be filled with tons of paragraphs of information.

Yes, it's important to cover the whole history; however, sometimes it's nice to see just the main keypoints without digging through paragraphs and paragraphs of information to find what you're looking for.

Three Goals:

First off, I want to discuss the overview and broad history of Nintendo home consoles.

Secondly, it's very important to cover these past consoles, because of the legacy and impact each piece of hardware has left in the gaming industry.

Lastly, knowing who created it and when these consoles came to tuition is a very important piece on the success of a console in the market.

Site Inspiration:

The site, "VGMueseum" is very important towards the creation of my website.

The reason for VGMueseum's inspiration is mainly, because of the connection and love towards retro games.

As mentioned before, the site VGMueseum covers a wide variety of retro game facts and media; however, we decided to take the direction of only going through some of Nintendo's past consoles.

The layout of VGMueseum will have a lot of connections to the organization and layout of my site, with the exception of my site covering the past Nintendo consoles.

Content:

As mentioned before, this website will cover a brief history on the NES,SNES, and Nintendo 64.

In a general perspective, this site will cover the launch date of the console, first and last game released, best and worst selling game and original retail price.

Also, there will be a section on each page that covers everything; but, in the other well-known regions such as, Japan and the PAL region(Europe and Australia).

Target Audience:

This website will target the gamers or people who love all sorts of video games.

When it's all said and done, my site can appeal to anyone, because of how gaming has became so mainstream.
